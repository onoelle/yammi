News about Yammi

29.03.2007 - Yammi development stopped...

Due to the lack of time to continue my work on Yammi I have to announce that development of Yammi has stopped (not without releasing a little bugfix version, see the entry below).

Though I'm still missing my favourite feature of Yammi (fast and simple fuzzy search) in Amarok, I can easily recommend those of you who are still using Yammi, but are missing essential features or experiencing problems, to switch to Amarok...

Thanks for all the feedback and all contributions to this project, I learned a lot from it...

...and if I find some time, I might spend it on integrating a decent fuzzy search into Amarok (there's a feature request for it, and if you miss it too, you can vote for it).

29.03.2007 - Version 1.2.3 released

As I stopped development of Yammi I did not want to leave behind a version that does not even compile on recent gcc versions (4.0 and later), so here is a minimal bugfix version with just two bugs fixed:

  • fixed: incorrect qualifier in "fuzzsrch.h" causing compilation problems with newer versions of gcc (4.0 and above)
  • fixed: Bug 1436536: bug in consistency check (not detecting two songs with identical primary keys)
As usual, see the DOWNLOAD section for downloading.



03.11.2005 - Article about Yammi in Linux Magazine

The Linux Magazine features an article about Yammi in its December 2005 Issue (No. 61). The closing paragraph concludes: "Yammi keeps the promise of being simple and compact. [...] Yammi is good choice for those who are put off by the feature overkill that databases such as Amarok [7] give but still need more than just a simple media player."

18.01.2005 - Version 1.2-rc2 released

The long-awaited integration of Scott Wheeler's taglib made me release another release candidate. Some more bugfixes and little improvements make Yammi more usable, for a detailed list see the CHANGELOG. One point should be mentioned for upgraders: The consistency mode settings have been changed, see the tooltips in the settings or the handbook for details.
